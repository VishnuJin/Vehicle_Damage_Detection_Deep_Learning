How it works

Car Damage allows you to upload a picture of your car damage to independently assess damage location and severity, easing the burden of filing an insurance claim and getting your car repaired.

camera_enhance

SNAP

Take a picture of your car damage.For best results, try to focus on one major area of damage(e.g:front,side, or rear) and contain most of the car body in the picture.

cloud_upload

SUBMIT

Upload your picture to Car Damage Assessor using our handy web app and image upload capabilities.

settings_applications

ASSESS

Deep learning model has been builtusing Google's AutoML Vision API, this will identify where and how severely your car is damaged and provide an immediate assessment of your damage.

receipt

RESULTS

Results can be used to obtain a cost estimate or submit to your insurance company to expedite claims handling.
